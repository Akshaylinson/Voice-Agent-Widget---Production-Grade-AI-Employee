🎙️ AI Voice Assistant Demo

Welcome to Codeless AI's intelligent voice assistant! Our AI-powered agent can answer questions about our services, pricing, and company information using natural conversation.

📋 How to Test:

  1. Look for the floating avatar in the bottom-right corner
  2. Click the avatar to activate the voice assistant
  3. Allow microphone access when prompted by your browser
  4. Listen to the introduction - our AI will greet you
  5. Speak your question clearly after the introduction ends
  6. Wait for the response - the AI will answer using our knowledge base
  7. Continue the conversation - ask follow-up questions!

💬 Try These Sample Questions:

"What services do you offer?"
"Tell me about your pricing plans"
"How does your AI voice agent work?"
"What is Codeless AI?"
"How can I contact support?"
"What makes your company different?"
